## One CMS
One CMS is unified content management system, specifically crafted for use across organization digital platforms. It serves as a centralized tool for viewing and managing content across both internal and external websites or platforms operated by organization. 

### Functionality:
* A comprehensive tool for managing content on all internal and external digital platforms associated with organization. The system features personalized access levels, ensuring that users receive privileges and capabilities tailored to their specific roles and responsibilities within the organization. 
* Addresses the issue of having multiple, separate content viewing and management systems that previously required several logins to access and were not integrated. 

By consolidating these systems into a single, unified platform, One CMS simplifies the process of content management and improves efficiency by allowing staff to access all resources with a single login, thereby enhancing workflow integration across the organization. Made with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/), [Fiber](https://gofiber.io/), [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/), [Typescript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)
