# Selection Projects

## Macro monitor
A platform for real-time insights and analysis of the Indonesian economy, delivering up-to-date and accurate economic data to inform strategic planning and decision-making. It provides a deeper understanding of Indonesia's economic landscape to support your economic monitoring needs with precision and ease. Made with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/), [Fiber](https://gofiber.io/), [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/), [Typescript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/), [Apexcharts.js](https://apexcharts.com/)

## One CMS
One CMS is unified content management system, specifically crafted for use across organization digital platforms. It serves as a centralized tool for viewing and managing content across both internal and external websites or platforms operated by organization. The system features personalized access levels, ensuring that users receive privileges and capabilities tailored to their specific roles and responsibilities within the organization. Made with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/), [Fiber](https://gofiber.io/), [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/), [Typescript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)

## Virgo
A hassle-free, smart, and human-friendly virtual assistant that assists you with all your data needs. Virgo modernizes and democratizes your knowledge, making data management and access easier and more efficient for everyone. Made with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/), [Fiber](https://gofiber.io/), [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/), [Typescript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/), [Vertex AI](https://cloud.google.com/vertex-ai?hl=en)

## RustScrappy
A platform specifically designed to handle web scraping tasks efficiently, processing and storing of data extracted from various websites. Made with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/), [Typescript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/), [Actix Web](https://actix.rs/), [PostgreSQL](https://www.postgresql.org/)
